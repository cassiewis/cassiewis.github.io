Our Ingredients

My handcrafted candles are made from the finest wax found on the great lands of this earth. Infused with pink himalayan salt, my candles have the astonishing smell of a newborn baby and the silky texture of a fish's scales. They pack a powerful punch to the senses, but not to your health. We offer all you could ever hope for in a clean burning, long-lasting, twine-wicked candle.
